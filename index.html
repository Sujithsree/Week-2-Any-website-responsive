ABOUT US

Frootle India proudly serves as the exclusive partner to leading international brands in India, specializing in Lifestyle, Wellness, and Home & Kitchen sectors'. Whether you prefer the convenience of online shopping or the personalized touch of our offline stores, we're here to cater to your needs every step of the way. From expert guidance before you make a purchase to seamless home installation once you've chosen your product, and ongoing lifetime support, we've got you covered. With over 300+ dedicated team members spread across the nation, our mission is simple: to ensure every customer's experience is nothing short of delightful.

To prioritize our customer's comfort, our products are readily accessible through both online and offline platforms.

ONLINE PRESENCE

Our products are readily available on major online platforms such as Amazon, Flipkart, Croma, Nykaa Fashion, Tata Cliq, First Cry, and more. Additionally, you can find our products on each brand's website for added convenience.

OFFLINE PRESENCE

In addition to our exclusive Frootle stores and partnerships with retailers, our products are also placed in offline markets, including Corma.This ensures heightened product visibility, instilling greater confidence in customers when making their purchase decisions.

OUR SERVICES

At Frootle, customer satisfaction is our top priority. With over 300 dedicated employees across India, each equipped with the required skill set, our primary goal is to provide a delightful experience to every customer.
